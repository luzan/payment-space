# KYC & KYB Verification Quiz

> **Status:** Pending content development

Test your understanding of identity verification, business validation, beneficial ownership, and sanctions screening.

## Questions (To Be Developed)

### KYC Requirements

1. What is the difference between KYC and KYB?
2. Which individuals at a merchant must undergo KYC verification?
3. What are acceptable forms of government-issued ID for KYC?
4. Why is liveness detection important for remote identity verification?

### KYB Requirements

5. What documents are required to verify an LLC?
6. How do you validate an EIN?
7. What is a Certificate of Good Standing and why does it matter?
8. What red flags should you look for when verifying a business entity?

### Beneficial Ownership

9. What is the ownership threshold that triggers beneficial owner identification?
10. When must you identify a "control person"?
11. Are publicly traded companies subject to beneficial ownership requirements?
12. How do you handle multi-tiered ownership structures (parent/subsidiary)?

### Sanctions Screening

13. What is the OFAC SDN list?
14. Who must be screened against sanctions lists during merchant onboarding?
15. How often should sanctions screening be performed?
16. What is a Politically Exposed Person (PEP) and how does it affect onboarding?
17. What should you do if you discover a true positive sanctions match?

### Scenario-Based Questions

18. **Scenario:** A sole proprietor applies for a merchant account using a DBA name. What verification is required?

19. **Scenario:** An LLC has three owners: Alice (40%), Bob (35%), and Carol (25%). Who needs to undergo KYC verification?

20. **Scenario:** A corporation has five shareholders, but none owns more than 20% individually. What must you do for beneficial ownership compliance?

21. **Scenario:** During sanctions screening, you find a name match for one of the beneficial owners. The name and birthdate match, but the address is different. How do you proceed?

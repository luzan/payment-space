# Week 1-2: Resources & Reading Materials

## Official Documentation

### Card Networks

- **Visa Core Rules and Visa Product and Service Rules**
    - Focus on: merchant acceptance requirements, PayFac requirements
    - Chapter 5 : Acceptance
    - Chapter 7 : Transaction Processing
    - [PDF: Visa Rules Public](https://usa.visa.com/content/dam/VCOM/download/about-visa/visa-rules-public.pdf)

- **Mastercard Rules Manual**
    - Focus on: Payment Facilitator requirements, merchant categories
    - Chapter 5: Acquiring Activity
    - Chapter 7: Service Providers and Network Enablement Partners
    - [PDF: Mastercard Rules](https://www.mastercard.us/content/dam/public/mastercardcom/na/global-site/documents/mastercard-rules.pdf)

### Industry Bodies

- **Electronic Transactions Association (ETA)**
    - Website: [electran.org](https://electran.org)
    - Look for: Payment Facilitator guidelines, industry whitepapers
    - [PDF: ETA Payment Facilitator Guidelines](https://electran.org/wp-content/uploads/ETA-Payment-Facilitator-Guidelines-TOC-ONLY.pdf)
    - ASK for complete document from electran.org with the business. Needs an account.

## Recommended Reading

### Books

- "Payments Systems in the U.S." by Carol Coye Benson and Scott Loftesness
    - Comprehensive overview of payment rails
    - Good foundation for understanding the ecosystem

### Online Resources

- **Stripe's Payment Fundamentals Guide**
    - [stripe.com/guides](https://stripe.com/guides)
    - Well-written explanations of payment concepts

- **Adyen's Payments Knowledge Hub**
    - [adyen.com/knowledge-hub](https://adyen.com/knowledge-hub)
    - Practical, implementation-focused content

## API Documentation to Study

Study these not for implementation, but to understand what data payment systems work with:

- **Stripe Connect Documentation**
    - Focus on: account types, money flow, platform models
    - [docs.stripe.com/connect](https://docs.stripe.com/connect)

- **Adyen for Platforms**
    - Focus on: split payments, sub-merchant structure
    - [docs.adyen.com/platforms](https://docs.adyen.com/platforms)

- **Square Developer Documentation**
    - Focus on: OAuth merchant onboarding, payment flows
    - [developer.squareup.com](https://developer.squareup.com)

## Research Keywords

When searching for information, use these terms:

- "Four party card payment model"
- "Interchange fee structure"
- "Payment facilitator vs ISO"
- "Card network authorization flow"
- "Merchant acquiring process"
- "Open loop vs closed loop payments"
- "Payment aggregator model"

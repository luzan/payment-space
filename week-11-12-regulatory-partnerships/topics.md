# Week 11-12: Topics to Research

## Week 11: Sponsor Banks & Card Network Registration

### 11.1 Sponsor Bank Fundamentals

**Research Focus:**

- What a sponsor bank is and why it's required
- Sponsor bank responsibilities in the PayFac model
- What sponsor banks provide (BINs, settlement, network access)
- What sponsor banks require (capital, compliance, insurance)
- Sponsor bank selection criteria
- Major sponsor banks in the PayFac space

**Key Terms to Define:**

- Sponsor bank / Acquiring bank
- BIN sponsorship
- Settlement account
- Reserve requirements
- Sponsor bank agreement

### 11.2 Sponsor Bank Relationship Structure

**Research Focus:**

- Typical sponsor bank agreement terms
- Revenue sharing models with sponsors
- Processing volume commitments
- Reserve and collateral requirements
- Reporting obligations to sponsor
- Audit rights and oversight
- Termination clauses and consequences

**Key Negotiation Points:**

- Pricing structure (interchange plus vs. flat rate)
- Reserve percentage and release schedule
- Volume minimums and maximums
- Prohibited merchant categories
- Liability allocation

### 11.3 Capital Requirements

**Research Focus:**

- Minimum capital requirements for PayFac registration
- Working capital needs for operations
- Reserve account funding
- Insurance requirements (cyber, E&O, crime)
- Capital adequacy ongoing requirements
- Investor considerations for PayFac businesses

**Financial Requirements to Understand:**

- Net worth requirements
- Liquid asset requirements
- Reserve funding obligations
- Insurance coverage minimums
- Bonding requirements (if applicable)

### 11.4 Visa PayFac Registration

**Research Focus:**

- Visa Payment Facilitator registration requirements
- Visa Third Party Agent (TPA) requirements
- Registration process and timeline
- Annual renewal requirements
- Ongoing compliance obligations
- Visa monitoring and reporting requirements

**Key Visa Requirements:**

- PCI DSS compliance certification
- Financial stability requirements
- Compliance program requirements
- Risk management requirements
- Merchant monitoring obligations

### 11.5 Mastercard PayFac Registration

**Research Focus:**

- Mastercard Payment Facilitator Program requirements
- Registration process differences from Visa
- Mastercard Registered Payment Facilitator status
- Annual certification requirements
- Mastercard-specific compliance requirements
- MATCH file reporting obligations

**Key Mastercard Requirements:**

- Registration documentation
- Compliance certifications
- Sub-merchant registration
- Risk program requirements

### 11.6 Other Network Considerations

**Research Focus:**

- American Express OptBlue program
- Discover network requirements
- Debit network requirements (PIN debit)
- International network considerations
- Network-specific rules and fees

**Research Focus:**

- What activities require MTL licensing
- Federal vs. state licensing framework
- When PayFacs need MTL vs. when they don't
- The "agent of payee" exemption
- Holding funds and timing considerations

**Key Terms to Define:**

- Money Transmitter License (MTL)
- Money Services Business (MSB)
- Agent of payee exemption
- Prepaid access
- Currency exchange

### 12.2 State Licensing Requirements

**Research Focus:**

- Number of states requiring licensing
- State-by-state variations in requirements
- Capital/surety bond requirements by state
- Application process and timeline
- Renewal and ongoing requirements
- Examination and audit requirements

**Key States to Research:**

- New York (BitLicense if crypto involved)
- California
- Texas
- Florida
- States with highest requirements

- Recordkeeping requirements
